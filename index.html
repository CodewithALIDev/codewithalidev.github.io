Granular Lifecycle Pipeline Breakdowns

Purchase Order (PO)

Initial commercial data node mapping explicitly defined procurement requisitions, lot validations, pricing parameters, and vendor contractual constraints directly into the schema tables.

Delivery Order (DO)

Validates outbound logistics parameters against source parameters, verifying consignment allocations, serial distributions, and route clearances before dispatch execution.

Inspection Protocol

An intermediary programmatic checkpoint enforcing high-fidelity physical testing rules, tracking pass/fail item metrics, deviations, and quantitative quarantine records.

Good Receive Note (GRN)

The core stock-ingestion engine that increments relational data tables upon validated delivery acceptance, initiating immediate general stock ledger updating routines.

GRN Return Note

Reverses erroneous or failed materials from inventory balances, triggering background adjustment scripts to align stock states and outstanding vendor tallies.

Material Issue Note

Authorizes and logs precise internal item consumptions, decrementing central stores based on department requests and allocation limits.

Issue Return Note

Safeguards against operational waste by allowing unused items to safely re-enter storage parameters under precise material tracking controls.

Pre-Service PO

An upfront scheduling framework tracking estimated labor costs, technical equipment definitions, and operational scope boundaries before final execution commitments.

Service PO Entry

Manages active non-material contracts, tracking mill maintenance operations, service fulfillment bounds, and direct cost allocations.

5-Store Authorized Control

Maintains five isolated operational storage divisions. Implements strict row-level profile security rules and multi-tier manager permission thresholds to govern multi-million unit assets safely.

5 Independent Inventories

Segregates transactional balances across unique storage configurations, offering independent ledger tracking, individual load capacities, and isolated audit paths.

Intelligence & Aging Analytics

Compiles complex database views to compute real-time structural data, from accurate Inventory Balances down to deep Supplier Outstandings and chronological payment Aging Reports.
