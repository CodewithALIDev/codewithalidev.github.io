Interconnected ERP Modules Breakdown

1. Order Management System

Handles commercial sales workflow, tracks customer purchase parameters, and feeds requirements directly into downstream mill production timelines.

2. Warping and Sizing

Monitors initial beam-preparation processes, yarn preparation stages, and calculates exact technical sizing elements required before looming.

3. Loom Production

Tracks active loom operational states, manages raw weaving plant outputs, and measures actual efficiencies against mill forecasts.

4. Processing

Tracks fabric chemical treatments, dyeing specifications, and finishing stages to guarantee industrial quality metrics are met consistently.

5. Fabric Inventory

8. Stitching

9. General Inventory

Maintains spare mill mechanics, tooling assets, consumables, and general hardware logs necessary to keep the facilities running smoothly.

10. Payroll
